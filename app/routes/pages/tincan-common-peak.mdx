This is the turnaround point for most people when ascending the [Tincan Uptrack](./tincan-uptrack).

To reach the real summit, [Tincan Proper Peak](./tincan-proper-peak), you need to
bootpack along the sometimes-knifeedge ridge for another 15-25 minutes.
