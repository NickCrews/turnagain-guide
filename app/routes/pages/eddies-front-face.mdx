When skiing back down, the lowest angles are on the lookers left side,
near the skin track. You can get down here without exposing yourself to
avalanche terrain, and this is a great place to go on a high danger day.

The steeper lines are on the lookers right side.
They aren't super large or connected, but the individual
rollovers can be steep,
and <Link href="https://www.cnfaic.org/observations/#/view/observations/04c54368-a34b-4ffd-a230-4294de7bf6d1"> can have significant avalanches</Link>.
